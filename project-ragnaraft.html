Description

Ragnaraft is a 2D local top-down mobile game where two players are cooperating in a boat on the river, avoiding obstacles and other hazards to reach the beautiful damsel.

The players' goal is to get to the mouth of the river, reaching the damsel. To do this they will have to work together, collect the coin for the shop, moving and avoiding various obstacles (vertical, horizontal, individual and atmospheric). The game has a single level, the sections of which are randomized to make the challenge as varied and re-playable as possible. The increasing complexity of the level challenge is determined by the positioning of the obstacles and their combination.
