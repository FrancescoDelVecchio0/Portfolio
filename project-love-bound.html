Love Bound

Unity | Team Project | Jam

Overview

Love Bound is a small game developed in 1 week during a jam.

An endless game in 3D with a 1950’s aesthetic, where the player controls a married couple bound together by an unbreakable love ribbon.

Run as you can do, avoid and jump obstacles, get a new record.

Playthrough

Download (Google Drive)

Structure

The goal is to reach the greatest possible distance without dying in each run.
