The Last Coin

Unreal Engine 5 | Team Project | Jam

Overview

The Last Coin is a small game developed in 1 week during a jam.

Trapped in a dark gambling nightmare you have to face your addiction. Walk through a neverending nightmare and confront your Fate at the Slot Machine. Inspect the Panic Room in a desperate need for coins, but pay attention to your fragile Sanity. The Last Coin is a survival horror first-person game where you escape from the addiction of grambling.

Find the way to escape to addiction!

Structure

Find coins for the slot machine to win enough coins to escape this nightmare before losing your sanity.

My Role

Worked as a generalist during a 1-week game jam, interaction system, coin system, UI for Sanity and Luck, and supporting the team across multiple areas as needed. The game was completed in just 1 week.
