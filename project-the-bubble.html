The Bubble

Unity | 1-Week Game | Team Project | Jam

Overview

The card is a small game developed in 1 week during a jam of Game Jam 2025 with theme card.

In first-person puzzle game, a pulsating green light echoes in this structure, you are not alone. The card is a survival horror game where you take part in the escape of the last member of a space crew from a mysterious entity.

Find the way to escape the card and reach spaceship!

Download (Google Drive)

Puzzle Structure

Find the keys for the doors and the fuses for generator, then reach the spaceship to escape, hide from Entity and avoid the cards.
