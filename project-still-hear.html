Still Hear

Unreal Engine 5 | Team Project

Overview

Still Hear is a game developed in 6 months during the second academic year.

In this third-person stealth puzzle game, you control the last Sound Keeper and a luminous being, one of the last sounds still alive, in a world where sound has been forgotten. Combine their unique abilities to solve puzzles, escape the Sound Eaters, and restore lost harmony
