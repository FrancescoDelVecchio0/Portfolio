My Career

ITT and Ettore Molinari Scientific High School

I graduated as an IT expert in 2020, obtaining certifications in Packet Trace and learning the C++ and Python languages. My first significant role was at MediaCom in Milan, a Call Center company. I quickly moved from a basic call center to managing IT systems, file documentation, and contract management via the company website.

Game for business

After graduating as an IT expert in 2020, I was approached to take a three-part training course, and I chose the Game for Business course. It was my first foray into the video game industry and the beginning of my true passion.

The Path to Game Dev

At Shibboleth,we wanted to create a video game together, but I didn’t yet have the necessary skills to make it happen. That’s when they introduced me to the Digital Bros Game Academy where my true passion was rekindled. Creating interactive prototypes and combining logic with creativity made me realize that game development is where I belong. I am currently refining these skills at the Digital Bros Game Academy in Milan, focusing on Unity and Unreal Engine.
